Children's Aid Society Cases


Amid Family Legal issues, the safety, health and well-being of children at home is sometimes compromised. In many cases, it is necessary to legally intervene and protect a child or children. Assistance to the family is also provided to help make the home a safe and nurturing environment for the children. Child Protection Laws in Ontario are designed to take children away from dangerous situations. In addition, families can obtain access to practical resources covering the areas of counseling, parenting classes, housing and drug or alcohol dependency treatment.

Our goal is to protect your children from danger and ensure that they are well cared for and are safe. We provide legal advice and knowledge to help educate Child Protective Agencies and Parents, familiarizing them with the laws advocating for the safety and well-being of children in the GTA. We can also help Parents obtain necessary resources to help create a nurturing and safe environment for the entire family.
